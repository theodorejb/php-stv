Timetable

Based upon PHP 7.4 release schedule

  • Tag on Tuesday, release on Thursday
  • 6 months pre-release phase
Date Release
Jun 25 2020 Alpha 1 Announce Files
Jul 09 2020 Alpha 2 Announce Files

Sep 17 2020 Beta 4 Announce Files
Oct 01 2020 RC 1 Announce Files
Oct 15 2020 RC 2 Announce Files
Oct 29 2020 RC 3 Announce Files
Nov 12 2020 RC 4 Announce Files
Nov 19 2020 RC 5 Announce Files
Nov 26 2020 GA

Note that RC6 has been removed to align the schedule with the release of other active branches. 8.0.0's GA date of Nov 26th coincides with the scheduled releases of 7.4.13 and 7.3.25
