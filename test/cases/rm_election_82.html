Voting

There are seven candidates for two available seats. While it might not be likely that all preferences are necessary, you are encouraged to use all votes. If you vote for the same person twice, this will not help that person's chances, it will only throw away one of your votes and deprioritize the remaining ones, so please cast unique votes.

Voting using STV (Single Transferable Vote) started on May 11, 2022. Voting will be open until May 18, 2022. Tie breaking will be at the discretion of the prior branch RMs.
